Wealth Inequality on the Rise in US

by Shane Loewe

Right now, America is tense. Amidst the global pandemic, political polarization is at an all time high and it is difficult for us as a society to agree on anything, let alone our government leaders agreeing with each other on anything. Although, despite the polarization, there is one thing that is sure to scare any American, or 95% of Americans, rather. Income inequality in America has been rising steadily since the 1980’s and doesn’t show any signs of slowing down, this means that as working and middle class household income is decreasing or remaining stagnant, the wealthy keep getting wealthier.
