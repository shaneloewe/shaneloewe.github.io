Our Reproductive Rights are Our Human Rights

The Center's Misson

Every person has a right to participate as an equal member of society regardless of gender, which means that women should be free to decide whether or when to have children or get married. Access to reproductive health care should be guaranteed so that every woman can make these decisions free of discrimination.
